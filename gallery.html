Where We Start

We begin with the highest quality raw materials, carefully sourced from trusted suppliers. Our materials undergo initial quality checks before entering the production process.

Extrusion

Extrusion process ensures consistent thickness, high strength, and flexibility, making it ideal for producing large volumes of high-quality plastic film products efficiently.

Quality Test 1

Plastic cover is carefully inspected for thickness uniformity, clarity, and strength, Any defects such as air bubbles, uneven texture, or impurities are identified to ensure the film meets quality standards for durability and transparency before moving to the next process.

Printing

Designs, logos, or product details are printed onto the plastic film using high-quality inks and precision rollers, ensuring clear, durable, and attractive prints that enhance the productâ€™s appearance and brand identity.

Cutting & Sealing

The printed plastic film is precisely cut to the required sizes and heat-sealed to form bags or covers, ensuring strong, leak-proof edges and uniform dimensions for the final product.

Quality Test 2

Finished transparent plastic covers undergo a thorough inspection for overall quality, including clarity, strength, proper dimensions, sealing integrity, and defect-free surfaces. Only products that meet all quality standards are approved for packaging and delivery.

Packaging

Finished products are carefully packaged with protective materials and prepared for shipping. Our logistics network ensures timely delivery to customers worldwide.

Delivery

Our efficient delivery system ensures your products arrive on time and in perfect condition. We partner with trusted logistics providers for reliable worldwide shipping.
