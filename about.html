First Major Expansion

Our Safety Commitment

Protecting our people, products, and planet

Workplace Safety

Our manufacturing facilities adhere to OHSAS 18001 standards with regular safety audits, emergency preparedness drills, and comprehensive PPE for all employees.

Staff Training

All employees undergo rigorous safety training programs with quarterly refreshers. Our operators receive specialized machine safety certifications.

Product Safety

All materials are FDA-approved and food-grade where applicable. We conduct regular batch testing for chemical composition and durability.

Environmental Safety

Zero liquid discharge policy with advanced effluent treatment. Our energy recovery systems reduce carbon footprint by 30% compared to industry standards.
